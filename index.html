Richmond volleyball: How 'one percent' is paying off

RICHMOND, Ind. â€” The Richmond High School volleyball team had a rough end to the month of August. The Red Devils lost four straight matches to Delta, Greenfield-Central, Hagerstown and Triton Central, and didn't win a single set in that span. With a first-year head coach and more than enough youth to go around, with only four seniors, Richmond could've packed it in and chalked this season up to a young team. Head coach Russ Uhte and the Red Devils would do no such thing. Over the past two weeks, Richmond has won seven of its last nine matches. The Red Devils are playing their best volleyball of the season, and their hot streak culminated with senior night on Monday, when Richmond swept Rushville in dominant fashion in front of an energetic Tiernan Center crowd.
